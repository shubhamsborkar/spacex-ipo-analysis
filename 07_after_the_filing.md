# ⚠️ NOT FROM THE S-1 — Events After the Filing Was Frozen (Public News, Unverified)

> **Read this first.** Nothing in this file comes from the SpaceX Form S-1 analyzed in files [[01_revenue_growth]] through [[06_anthropic_compute_deal]]. The S-1 I analyzed was the preliminary prospectus **filed May 20, 2026**. A registration statement is a snapshot frozen on its filing date — anything **decided or announced after May 20, 2026 cannot be in it**. The two items below happened *after* that date, so they are sourced separately to **dated public news** and are kept in this separate file. **Do not blend these figures with the S-1.** All numbers here are from public reporting, **not the filing, and should be verified against the final/amended SEC filing before being relied on.**

**Method note:** This is the "what changed after the document was frozen" human check — the step no document-reading tool can do for you. A tool can only read the page in front of it; it cannot know the world moved on after the page was printed.

**Figures policy:** Every figure below is reproduced exactly as supplied from public news reports. No numbers were invented, estimated, or derived. Where a figure is described as "about" or "roughly," that hedge is carried over from the reporting.

## 3. Why This Matters

- The S-1 analysis correctly reported the IPO price as **"not disclosed"** ([[05_valuation]]) — and that was *accurate as of the filing*. The price existed only *after* June 3, 2026. Reporting it as disclosed would have been wrong; ignoring that it later appeared would leave the picture stale. The fix is exactly this: a clearly-labeled, separately-sourced, dated file.
- The S-1 documented the **Anthropic** compute deal ([[06_anthropic_compute_deal]]) but could not contain the **Google** deal, which was announced 16 days later. Both are real; only one is in the filing.
- **Rule of thumb:** filing facts → cite the S-1 page. Post-filing facts → cite dated news *and* keep them out of the filing analysis until confirmed against the final/amended SEC filing.
