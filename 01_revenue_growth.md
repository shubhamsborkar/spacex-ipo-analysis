# SpaceX S-1: Revenue — Verified

**Source:** Space Exploration Technologies Corp. Form S-1 (audited consolidated financial statements for fiscal years ended December 31, 2025, 2024, 2023; PCAOB-audited).

**Method:** Every number in the prior revenue analysis was re-verified by re-reading the S-1 from scratch (statements, segment note, MD&A tables, prospectus summary). Reconciliations: each segment total is checked against the consolidated revenue line.

**Segment naming:** The S-1 names three reportable segments — **Space, Connectivity, AI** (mapped here to "Launch", "Starlink/connectivity", "AI/xAI"). The 2023 and 2024 segment data are recast to this structure following the xAI and X Holdings mergers (treated as common-control transactions; see Note 19, p. F-57).

---

## 1. Total Revenue — Consolidated Statements of Operations (p. F-5)

| Year | Revenue ($M) | Citation |
|---|---:|---|
| 2025 | 18,674 | S-1, Consolidated Statements of Operations, p. F-5 |
| 2024 | 14,015 | S-1, Consolidated Statements of Operations, p. F-5 |
| 2023 | 10,387 | S-1, Consolidated Statements of Operations, p. F-5 |

All three figures confirmed on second pass.

## 2. Revenue by Segment — Note 19, "Segments" (p. F-57 to F-59)

| Year | Space ($M) | Connectivity ($M) | AI ($M) | Total ($M) | Citation |
|---|---:|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 2025 | 4,086 | 11,387 | 3,201 | 18,674 | S-1, Note 19, p. F-58 |
| 2024 | 3,796 | 7,599 | 2,620 | 14,015 | S-1, Note 19, p. F-59 |
| 2023 | 3,557 | 3,869 | 2,961 | 10,387 | S-1, Note 19, p. F-59 |

**Reconciliation check:** All three segment rows sum to the consolidated total revenue for the respective year (verified: 4,086 + 11,387 + 3,201 = 18,674; 3,796 + 7,599 + 2,620 = 14,015; 3,557 + 3,869 + 2,961 = 10,387).

## 5. Not Disclosed in the S-1

- **Audited revenue for 2022 and earlier years:** Not disclosed. 2022 appears only as an opening balance in the Statement of Shareholders' Equity (p. F-7); no income statement or segment split is provided for any year before 2023.
- **Sub-segments (e.g., "Launch Services" vs. "Launch and Development" inside Space; "Starlink consumer" vs. "enterprise" vs. "Starshield" inside Connectivity; "X advertising" vs. "Grok subscription" vs. "data licensing" inside AI):** Not disclosed. The S-1 reports only at the three-segment level. MD&A text discusses sub-mix qualitatively (e.g., Connectivity consumer revenue grew 99.9% in 2025, p. 111) but provides no dollar split.
- **Q1 2025 stand-alone segment revenue:** Not disclosed in the audited financials. Q1 2025 figures appear only in the unaudited Q1 2026 vs. Q1 2025 comparison (Statements of Operations, p. F-64; MD&A, p. 4 and p. 89).
