# SpaceX S-1: Valuation Inputs & Multiples — Verified

**Source:** Space Exploration Technologies Corp. Form S-1, filed with the SEC on May 20, 2026 (preliminary prospectus, "SUBJECT TO COMPLETION, DATED, 2026"). PCAOB-audited consolidated financials for FY2025/2024/2023; unaudited interim data as of and for the three months ended March 31, 2026.

**Method:** Every number below is taken from the S-1 only. After first extraction, each cited page was re-read from scratch to confirm the figure and the page. Multiples are explicitly labeled **DERIVED** — none are stated in the filing. Trailing revenue and operating cash flow are the FY2025 figures already verified in [[01_revenue_growth]] and [[02_cash_flow]].

**Bottom line up front:** **The IPO price, the number of shares offered, and the dollar amount being raised are all NOT DISCLOSED** — they are blank placeholders in this preliminary prospectus. Because price is blank, **market capitalization, enterprise value, price/sales, and price/operating-cash-flow cannot be computed from the S-1.** What *is* disclosed is the full pre-IPO share count and capital structure (as of March 31, 2026). Those are the raw inputs below, plus the per-share revenue and cash-flow denominators a price would sit on, plus an explicitly-hypothetical sensitivity grid (clearly NOT from the filing) so the arithmetic is ready the moment a price is set.

## 5. Multiples — DERIVED (and why the headline ones are not computable)

**Price/Sales and Price/Operating-Cash-Flow both require market capitalization, which requires an IPO price. The price is NOT DISCLOSED.** Therefore P/S and P/OCF **cannot be computed from the S-1.** What follows is everything that *can* be derived, with the missing input clearly marked.

### 5a. The formulas, with denominators filled from the S-1

- Pre-IPO share base (pro forma A+B, p. 69): **12,520,310,769 shares**
- FY2025 revenue (p. F-5): **$18,674M**
- FY2025 CFO (p. F-8): **$6,785M**

| Multiple | Formula | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Market cap | IPO price × 12,520,310,769 (pre-IPO) + (price × new shares) | **Not computable — price & new-share count blank** |
| Enterprise value | Market cap + net debt $13,259M (pro forma, Mar 31 2026) | **Not computable — depends on market cap** |
| **P/S (FY2025)** | Market cap ÷ $18,674M | **Not computable — needs market cap** |
| **P/OCF (FY2025)** | Market cap ÷ $6,785M | **Not computable — needs market cap** |
| Revenue per pro-forma share (DERIVED) | 18,674 ÷ 12,520.31 = **$1.4915/share** | Computable |
| CFO per pro-forma share (DERIVED) | 6,785 ÷ 12,520.31 = **$0.5420/share** | Computable |

So, on a per-share basis: **P/S = IPO price ÷ $1.49** and **P/OCF = IPO price ÷ $0.54.** Plug in a price when one is set.

## 6. Reconciliation Notes — What Did Not Cleanly Reconcile on the Re-Read

1. **Price, shares offered, proceeds all blank** — confirmed on second read of the cover page, the cover offering table, p. 18 (Summary), and p. 66 (Use of Proceeds). This is normal for a "Subject to Completion" preliminary prospectus; it is the single biggest reason the headline valuation multiples cannot be computed from the filing.
2. **Two different period dates are being mixed.** The share count and capital structure are **as of March 31, 2026** (p. 69), while revenue and CFO are **FY2025** (year ended Dec 31, 2025). Trailing multiples conventionally pair the latest share count with trailing flows, but the mismatch is real and is flagged.
3. **Debt jumped $22.0B → $29.1B between Dec 31, 2025 and Mar 31, 2026.** [[03_debt]] shows $22,049M funded-debt principal at Dec 31, 2025 (net cash of $0.3B); the cap table shows $29,111M total long-term debt at Mar 31, 2026 (net debt $13.3B). The driver is the new **$20,000M SpaceX Bridge Loan** drawn in Q1 2026 (matures Sep 2, 2027; cap-table note 2) plus cash drawdown ($24.7B → $15.9B). An EV built on the Mar-2026 balance sheet is therefore ~$13.5B higher than one built on Dec-2025. Both are shown.
4. **Cash fell $24,747M (Dec 31, 2025, p. F-4) → $15,852M (Mar 31, 2026, p. 69).** Confirmed against both pages; consistent with $12.6B of Q1-2026 investing outflows referenced in MD&A.
5. **Share-count units shift between sections.** The audited balance sheet (p. F-4) and interim balance sheet (Mar 31, 2026) state shares in *millions* (e.g., Class A "2,036... 1,954" / "2,965... 2,883"), while the cap table (p. 69) states *exact* shares (2,882,444,444). They reconcile to the same figures once rounded; one cosmetic slip: Dec 31, 2025 Class A outstanding shows as 1,954M on the audited balance sheet (p. F-4) but 1,952M in the interim comparative (Mar 31, 2026 statement) — a 2M rounding difference, immaterial.
6. **Actual→pro-forma share bridge is not itemized** on the cap-table face (section 2b). Taken as disclosed; the conversion mechanics are not reconstructed.
7. No numeric discrepancy in the anchor figures: FY2025 revenue $18,674M (p. F-5) and CFO $6,785M (p. F-8) reconfirmed against [[01_revenue_growth]] and [[02_cash_flow]].
